### Rare Variant Hunting Steps

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build a Strong Core Team">
    Ensure your main battle team can comfortably handle any wild encounters or dungeons you plan to farm. This minimizes downtime and maximizes efficiency during your hunt.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Target Evomons">
    Decide which Shiny or Sparkle Evomon you want to hunt. Research its spawn locations and any specific conditions for its appearance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Catching Resources">
    Stock up on advanced catching balls and any items that might increase encounter rates or make catching easier. Don't waste your best resources on common Evomons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish a Farming Route">
    Create an efficient route through the Evomon's spawn area. Repeatedly traverse this route, engaging in encounters until you find your target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prepare for the Catch">
    When you encounter a Shiny or Sparkle Evomon, weaken it carefully without knocking it out. Use status effects like Sleep or Paralysis to increase your chances of a successful capture.
  </Step>
</Steps>
