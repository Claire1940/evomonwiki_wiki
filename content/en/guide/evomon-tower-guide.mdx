<Section icon={Target} title="Strategic Approaches to Dungeon Boss Fights">
Boss fights in Evomon towers require more than just raw power. Each boss has unique attack patterns, elemental types, and often specific weaknesses that can be exploited. Understanding these elements is key to victory.

<Callout type="info" title="Boss Battle Fundamentals">
Always prioritize surviving the boss's most powerful attacks. Use defensive buffs, debuffs on the boss, and heal when necessary. Don't be afraid to retreat and re-strategize if your current team isn't working.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Boss Type and Weaknesses">
    Before engaging, try to determine the boss's elemental type. This allows you to select Evomons with type advantage, significantly increasing your damage output and survivability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plan Your Skill Rotation">
    Many powerful skills have cooldowns. Plan which skills to use and when, especially for critical moments like breaking shields or dealing burst damage. Avoid wasting strong attacks when the boss is invulnerable or heavily shielded.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Health and Status Effects">
    Keep an eye on your Evomons' health bars. Use healing skills or items proactively. Be prepared to cure status effects like Poison or Burn, as they can quickly deplete your team's HP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Coordinate in Co-op">
    If playing with friends, communicate roles. One player might focus on healing, another on debuffing the boss, and the rest on dealing damage. Coordinated attacks can make short work of even the toughest bosses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Adapt and Overcome">
    If your initial strategy fails, analyze what went wrong. Did you lack elemental coverage? Was your healing insufficient? Adjust your team composition or strategy for the next attempt.
  </Step>
</Steps>
