<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Leveling and Evolution">
Efficient leveling and strategic evolution are key Evomon tips and tricks to maximize your team's potential. EXP Fruits are valuable resources, but knowing where and when to use them makes all the difference. Focus on your core team members to ensure consistent power growth.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand the Core Loop">
    Master the basic cycle of wild encounters, catching, training, and turn-based battles. This understanding allows you to identify which Evomons are worth investing in.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose a Core Carry">
    Designate one battle-ready Evomon as your primary damage dealer for PvE and boss fights. A focused carry accelerates combat and provides a stable point for your team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic EXP Fruit Use">
    Apply Medium EXP Fruits to your chosen carry and other core team members that provide crucial elemental coverage. This ensures the fastest progression for your active lineup.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purposeful Battle Farming">
    Engage in repeated battles with a clear objective: raise specific levels, test new skills, or confirm an Evomon's readiness for harder content. Avoid aimless grinding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evolve for Team Value">
    Prioritize evolving Evomons that gain a clear, beneficial role in your active team. Evolution should enhance progression, dungeon readiness, or PvP pressure, not just for novelty.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Test in Harder Content">
    Regularly challenge map bosses, co-op dungeons, and PvP battles to identify any weaknesses in your leveled team. Harder content reveals gaps in coverage and underleveled picks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="EXP Fruit Allocation">
Always use EXP Fruits on Evomons you plan to keep in your main battle team. Spreading them across too many creatures, especially duplicates or untested catches, will slow down your overall progression.
</Callout>
