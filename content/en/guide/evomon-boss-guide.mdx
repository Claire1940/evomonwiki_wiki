<Section icon={Target} title="Strategic Skill Usage in Boss Fights">
Skills are your most potent tools in Evomon battles. Knowing when and how to use them can turn the tide of a difficult boss encounter. Effective skill timing involves understanding cooldowns, anticipating boss moves, and prioritizing targets.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze Boss Patterns">
    Observe the boss's attack animations and skill indicators. Many bosses have predictable sequences or telegraph powerful moves. Learning these patterns allows you to prepare your defensive or offensive responses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Target Focus">
    If the boss summons minions, decide whether to eliminate them first or focus all damage on the boss. Generally, clear dangerous minions that apply debuffs or deal high damage before returning to the main boss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Time Your Buffs/Debuffs">
    Apply offensive buffs to your main attacker and defensive buffs to your tank just before the boss unleashes a powerful attack. Similarly, use debuffs on the boss to reduce its damage or defense at critical moments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute High-Damage Skills">
    Unleash your most powerful offensive skills when the boss is debuffed, its shield is down, or your Evomon has an elemental advantage. Avoid wasting high-cooldown skills on low-impact turns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Manage Healing and Status Cleansing">
    Use healing skills proactively to keep your team healthy, especially after heavy boss attacks. Have Evomons ready to cleanse debilitating status effects like Poison, Burn, or Sleep, as these can cripple your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Some boss battles in Evomon might require specific Evomon abilities or type combinations to effectively counter their unique mechanics. Experiment with different team setups if a standard approach isn't working.
</Callout>
