<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Leveling, Evolution, and EXP Fruit Optimization">
Efficient leveling and strategic evolution are cornerstones of successful **Evomon progress**. EXP Fruits are valuable resources that can significantly accelerate this process, but they must be used wisely. The key is to focus your efforts on Evomons that contribute most to your battle effectiveness.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand the Core Loop">
    Begin by mastering how wild encounters, catching, training, and turn-based battles work. This foundational knowledge allows you to make informed decisions about which Evomons to invest in.
    <Callout type="tip">
    **Avoid:** Spending progression rewards blindly. Understand the strengths and weaknesses of your Evomons before committing resources.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Your Core Carry">
    Select one battle-ready Evomon to serve as your main damage dealer for PvE progression and boss encounters. This Evomon should receive priority in leveling.
    <Callout type="warning">
    **Avoid:** Leveling every new catch evenly in the early game. This creates a wide roster of mediocre Evomons rather than a strong, reliable team.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic EXP Fruit Usage">
    Allocate Medium EXP Fruits to your core carry and other key Evomons that provide essential elemental coverage. These fruits are most effective when used on active team members.
    <Callout type="info">
    **Why it matters:** EXP Fruits offer faster leveling when applied to Evomons consistently used in your active battle team, maximizing their impact on your overall **Evomon progress**.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purposeful Battle Farming">
    Engage in repeated battles with a clear objective: raise specific Evomon levels, test new skills, and confirm their readiness for harder content like bosses, dungeons, and PvP.
    <Callout type="warning">
    **Avoid:** Grinding random fights without assessing if your team's capabilities are actually improving or if you're just wasting time.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evolve for Team Value">
    Prioritize evolving Evomons that gain a clear, beneficial role for your active lineup after evolving. Evolution should enhance your team's overall battle performance.
    <Callout type="info">
    **Why it matters:** Evolution is most impactful when it directly contributes to progression, dungeon readiness, or PvP competitiveness, not just for novelty.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Test Your Team in Challenging Content">
    Regularly test your leveled team against map bosses, co-op dungeons, and PvP battles. This exposes weaknesses in coverage, underleveled picks, or skills that only seem effective in easy fights.
    <Callout type="tip">
    **Avoid:** Judging your team's strength solely based on easy wild encounters. True power is revealed in tougher challenges.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
