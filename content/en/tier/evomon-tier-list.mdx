Building a strong team in Evomon is a continuous process that evolves as you progress. From choosing your starter to optimizing for dungeons and PvP, each step is crucial.

  <Step num="2" title="Build Elemental Coverage Early">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identify and Train Your Core Carry">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Test and Refine Your Team in Hard Content">
