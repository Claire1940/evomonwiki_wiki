### A-Tier: Strategic Anchors
A-tier monsters are essential for surviving the "attrition" battles found in higher-level Dungeons. Unlike carries, these monsters focus on sustainability, status effects, and protecting your main damage dealer.

- **Dungeon-Ready Anchor**: High survival stats and skills that mitigate incoming damage.
- **Coverage Specialist**: Specifically trained to counter the types that threaten your S-tier carry.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolution and EXP Fruit Optimization">

To turn a basic catch into one of the **evomon top monsters**, you must master the evolution system. Leveling up through battles is the traditional way, but smart players use **EXP Fruits** to bypass the "weak" middle stages of an evolution chain.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Carry">
    Choose a monster with a three-stage evolution path. These typically have the highest endgame stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resource Hoarding">
    Save all **Medium EXP Fruits** from codes and milestone rewards. Do not waste them on early-game catches you won't keep.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Power Leveling">
    Feed the fruits to your chosen monster once it reaches level 15. This usually triggers the first major power spike.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Evolution">
    Continue focused training until the final evolution is unlocked. This monster now becomes your "Team Captain."
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Fruit Type | EXP Value | Best Usage | Rarity |

| **Small Fruit** | Low | Leveling new catches | Common |
| **Medium Fruit** | Moderate | Pushing to Evolution | Rare |

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Mistake">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Endgame Team Building & Dungeon Strategy">

Once you have secured several **evomon top monsters**, you must organize them into a functional team. A team of six Fire-types might look cool, but it will fail miserably in the Water-themed endgame Dungeons.

### The Winning Formula
A balanced team should follow the **1-2-3 Rule**:
1. **One Core Carry**: Your highest level, highest damage unit.
2. **Two Coverage Units**: Different elements to handle the Carry's weaknesses.
3. **Three Utility/Leveling Slots**: For monsters you are currently evolving or using for specific buffs.

| Team Role | Essential Skill | Recommended Type | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lead** | High Initiative | Fire / Electric | High |
| **Tank** | Self-Heal / Shield | Grass / Earth | Medium |
| **Finisher** | High Single-Target | Water / Dark | High |
