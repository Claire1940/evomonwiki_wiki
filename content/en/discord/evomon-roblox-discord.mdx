| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

**Team Building Strategy:**
- **The Core Carry**: This is usually your evolved starter or a high-stat legendary. They should handle 90% of wild encounters.
- **The Coverage Slot**: If your carry is Fire, your support should be Water or Grass to handle the carry's natural counters.
- **The Utility Slot**: An Evomon with status-inflicting skills (Sleep, Paralyze) to make catching rare variants easier.
