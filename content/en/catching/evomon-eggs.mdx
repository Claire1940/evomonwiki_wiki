Effective egg hatching and collection prioritization are crucial for maximizing your roster's potential without wasting resources. It’s important to understand the best practices for integrating new Evomons from eggs into your team and deciding which ones deserve your training investment.

  <Step num="1" title="Hatch First, Evaluate Second">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Compare Roles and Coverage">
    After hatching, compare the new Evomon's type, skills, and potential evolution path with your current roster. Does it offer a unique advantage or fill a weakness in your existing team?
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invest Wisely in Training">
    Only invest EXP Fruits and other training resources into Evomons that clearly improve your team's overall battle performance or contribute to a specific strategy for harder content like dungeons or PvP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prioritize New Roles Over Duplicates">

  </Step>
</Steps>
